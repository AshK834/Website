TV Shows

Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a Netflix original series that takes place in a small Indiana town in 1983. When a young boy vanishes, his friends, the town's police department, and a group of scientists must confront supernatural forces in order to get him back.

The Witcher

The Witcher is a fantasy series based on the book series of the same name. It follows the story of Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter who struggles to find his place in a world where people often prove more wicked than beasts.
